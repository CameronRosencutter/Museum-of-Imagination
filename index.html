Who are We?

We are a section of the Museum of Imagination, focusing on strange and unique buildings. Our museum is located deep in a foggy forest, away from loud, bustling streets.

What we do

Discover

We travel the world looking for strange buildings that seem to defy all known gravity. From the smallest neighborhoods to the biggest cities, we look everywhere.

Research

We communicate with the owners of these bizarre buildings and make a deal with them to learn more about them.

Design

We study the building intesively, figuring out what is the best way to integrate into our museum.

Implement

Fianlly, we implement it into the museum, whether it be a painting, sculpture, etc.
